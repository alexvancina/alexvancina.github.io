About Me

I'm a Systems Librarian with over 20 years of experience working in public libraries. Currently, I manage the Technology & Metadata Services Department at Helen Plum Library in Lombard, IL, overseeing IT and technical services operations. I received my MLIS from San Jose State University and a BS in Computer Information Systems from DeVry University. I also serve as President of the RAILS Board of Directors and President of the New Lenox Public Library District Board of Trustees.

All opinions here are my own and do not necessarily reflect those of my employer or any other organization.
