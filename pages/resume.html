Volunteer Work

Advisory Board, LACONI Technical Services Section (2023 - present)

The LACONI Technical Services Section was created in 1991 and the continued goal is to provide Technical Services Librarians, (and other interested persons), opportunities for continuing education, professional development, and networking with their colleagues.

Joined the Advisory Board in 2023.

Steering Committee, Wisconsin/Illinois Innovative Users Group (2022 - present)

The mission of the Wisconsin/Illinois Innovative Users Group (WILIUG) is to facilitate communication and to help create learning opportunities, both online and in-person, for Innovative libraries in the area. The group exists to connect area staff working with the Innovative system so that they may share their experiences, frustrations, ideas and training materials. To this end, the group hopes to help staff develop professionally and to make the best possible use of Innovative products.

Served as the Steering Committee's Chair-Elect from 2022-2023 and Chair from 2023-present.

Habitat Management, Forest Preserve District of Will County (August 2022 - present)

Work with Forest Preserve District staff and volunteers to maintain and restore preserves throughout Will County, primarily focusing on brush control and removal of invasive plants to maintain healthy and sustainable habitats for native plants and animals.

Emergency Services & Disaster Agency, Village of New Lenox (June 2010 - July 2021)

Provided support services for first responders during natural disasters, fire calls, vehicle accidents, and pre-planned community events. Trained in incident management, traffic control, security, weather spotting, radio communications, first aid, and CPR/AED use.
